KeyChords
=========

1. General (MOD + \)

q | shutdown
h | help
w | wifi
b | bluetooth
e | emojis
s | screenshot
c | save clipboard
v | retrieve from saved clipboard

2. Layout (MOD + l)

m | monocycle
t | tiling
f | change window from floating
x | floating
h | horizontal tiling
v | default stack layout

KeyBindings
===========

g             | toggle top bar
q             | kill client
=             | increase tiling gap
Shift+=       | get tiling gap to 0
-             | decrease tiling gap
0             | combining all tags
9             | seperate tags
Space         | dmenu
Right         | move to right window
Left          | move to left window
Shift+Right   | resize to right
Shift+Left    | resize to left
Return        | shell
Shift+Return  | open browser in default
Ctrl+Return   | open browser in Profile 2

F1  | get-info
F2  | decrease brightness
F3  | increase brightness
F4  | full screenshot

F8  | increase volume

F10 | pause song on moc
